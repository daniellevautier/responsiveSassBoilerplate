Delivery

Kentwell large sofa in Scarborough Baltic Sea

* Due to our factory shutdown week commencing 28th August, all orders will have an additional 2 weeks added to the lead time.

We're so keen for you to sit on our sofas that if you buy a sofa from our website, delivery takes around 6 to 8 weeks for our Classic British ranges and from as little as 7 days on our Can't Wait sofa collection. That's way faster than most of the high street, and at a better price too.

We do charge for delivery, but only £49 to anywhere in mainland England, Wales and Scotland. So it's the same if you live in Edinburgh, London or indeed Penzance. A fraction of what it costs us! For places that may take us longer to reach (see postcode list at bottom of page) just give us a call and we can give you a very reasonable quote. Offshore UK and Ireland (which is the Isle of Man, Isle of Wight, Guernsey, Jersey and of course Ireland) we can recommend a delivery partner who will carry out your delivery for you direct.

Delivery, Delivery Time slots and Access

Classic British Sofa Orders

About a week before we deliver we will ring you so we can arrange a convenient date. Everybody hates hanging around for deliveries. So we do delivery time slots to make it all hassle free. We therefore give you a second call, or if we have a mobile number, a text, the day before, to tell you the two hour time slot for your delivery. Should anything untoward occur and we are running late, the driver's assistant will of course ring you again to keep you informed. If you don't hear from the warehouse you can ring our delivery team on 020 8578 9494 the day before your sofa is due. Don't ring earlier than one day before your sofa is due to be delivered. We won't have the information!
